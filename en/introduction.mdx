## What is BrainBox?

BrainBox is an advanced AI-powered platform that transforms how you interact with your documents. It enables teams to efficiently manage, analyze, and extract insights from their documentation using state-of-the-art artificial intelligence.

## Getting Started

### 1. Access BrainBox

Visit [app.brainbox.com.co](https://app.brainbox.com.co) and sign in with your Google or Microsoft account.

### 2. Upload Your First Document

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add New">
    Use the "Add new" button in the top bar and select "Files"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Documents">
    Upload PDF files up to 4MB each (maximum 6 files at once)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for Processing">
    Documents are typically processed within 30 seconds
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 3. Create Your First Box

Boxes are collaborative workspaces where you can:

* Share documents with team members

* Ask questions about your documents

* Manage access permissions

* Keep conversations organized

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Asking Effective Questions">
    * Be specific and clear in your questions - Reference specific documents
      when needed - Use the "Ask Sources" toggle for document-based answers
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Document Organization">
    * Create a logical folder structure - Use descriptive file names - Organize
      related documents in Boxes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Team Collaboration">
    * Assign appropriate user roles - Share context when adding team members -
      Review sources for accurate information
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
